Open Educational Resources

January 15, 2020

For over 20 years SNOW: Inclusive Learning and Education has provided community outreach through open resources including current information about assistive and accessible technologies, inclusive learning strategies and workshops for educators, students, parents and guardians about differences in the classroom, accessibility, and learning with technology.

We've recently created a primer for the SNOW community on Open Educational Resources (OER) to help with finding, modifying, creating and using OER.

Floe is a project of the Inclusive Design Research Centre, OCAD University.

Funded by a grant from The William and Flora Hewlett Foundation.
